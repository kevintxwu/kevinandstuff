Unboard
Game Studio

Unboard brings the magic of tabletop games to the mobile world. I founded Unboard and participated in the Berkeley based accelerator Free Ventures in Fall 2014. We also interviewed with YC and The Thiel Fellowship.

View Site View YC Application

In Unboard, I got a contract for a massively popular game like The Resistance, I built a beautiful game like Epic Everything, I found a more powerful network than I ever expected, and more. I learned a thousand things everyday.

I personally designed the branding, website, and logo of Unboard. With it, I tried to convey a playful feel while keeping things modern and simple. There's a feel of technology to the brand of Unboard, as its mission is bringing the physical into the digital and electronic.

The extensive use of orange reinforces the our values of being playful and modern. In addition, each Unboard graphic utilized sharp corners and simple shapes, to give a pixel-like vibe. The overall identity was one that would attract not only consumers looking for fun, but anybody that was interested in improving the tabletop world for business or passion.

As the founder of Unboard, I also interviewed with Y Combinator and The Thiel Fellowship. Though Unboard ultimately was not accepted in either program, the overall experience was once again a unique learning experience.
