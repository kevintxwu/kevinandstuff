Unboard
Game Studio

Unboard brings the magic of tabletop games to the mobile world. We were in the UC Berkeley accelerator Free Ventures in Fall 2014.

View Site View YC app

I achieved a lot with Unboard. We got a contract for a massively popular game like The Resistance, we built a beautiful game like Epic Everything, I built a more powerful network than I ever expected, and more. However, I also sacrificed a lot for Unboard. I took a semester off, lived under constant stress, and used up countless weekend nights.

I personally created the branding, website, and logo of Unboard. With it, I tried to convey a playful feel while keeping things modern and simple. There's a feel of technology to the theme of Unboard as well, as its mission is bringing the physical into the digital.

The extensive use of orange again reinforces the message of playful and modern. In addition, each Unboard graphic utilized sharp corners and simple shapes, to give a pixel-like vibe. The overall identity was one that would attract not only consumers looking for fun, but anybody that was interested in improving the tabletop world for business or passion.

As the founder of Unboard, I also interviewed with Y Combinator and The Thiel Fellowship. Though Unboard ultimately was not accepted in either program, the experience of running a company has taught me a lot.
