Inspiration

A personal note: Picasso famously once said “Good artists copy, great artists steal.” I believe that the most important thing you can do when designing is studying and referencing the work of as many other designers as possible. Start by almost completely copying entire themes, but never stop stealing and developing other designers’ color schemes, font pairings, and layouts.
