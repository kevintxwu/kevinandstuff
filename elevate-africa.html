Elevate Africa
Crowdfunding System

Elevate Africa is a nonprofit that supports small businesses and entrepreneurs in the poorest regions of Africa. They needed a website and crowdfunding system to raise funding. I led a team of designers and developers to conceptualize and build that system.

View Site View Repo

With Elevate Africa, we set out to build a crowdfunding campaign with the spirit of adventure! We wanted donors to feel like they were adventuring across Africa, and helping and learning about communities along the way.

We based our general system off of the a proven system used by Charity: Water, where any individual can start a fundraising campaign for the Elevate Africa cause. They would begin their own adventure and as they approached their fundraising goal they would also approach the goal of their adventure.

Meanwhile, we also added some general essential features. We implemented Stripe for donations in both campaigns and in general, as well as some fancy javascript animations for each individual page. We also built a basic blogging system called "The Buzz" within the site itself. The general campaign system and site was built in the Ruby on Rails.

We met with Wes Wasson and Rachel Lataitis from Elevate Africa many times throughout the process, and iterated until we felt we had something that they could work with. We've since helped Elevate Africa transition the project maintenance to a software consulting agency called GPI Holding.
