Quotes

A short note on design philosophy based a selection of quotes.

Charles Eames

“Design depends largely on constraints.”

Paul Rand

“Don’t try to be original. Just try to be good.”

Edsger Dijkstra

“The ability of discerning high quality unavoidably implies the ability of identifying shortcomings.”

Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

“Perfection is achieved, not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.”

Lindon Leader

“I strive for two things in design: simplicity and clarity. Great design is born of those two things.”

Note

The most important thing these quotes can teach you: design is not about what’s cool or pretty, it’s about what's right. Be as simple as possible. Care more about your execution than your idea. Pixel push over experimenting. This is the only non-technical point I’ll make. This is the best way to think about design.
